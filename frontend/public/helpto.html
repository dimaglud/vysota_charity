Whom do we help / requests
Athletes // Climbers, rock climbers, trail runners, runners etc.
* The athlete should be the person who has a license from the relevant federation.
  • Equipment for competitions and training
  • Financial support for participation in competitions
  • Financisal support for training
  • Financial support for other requests

Clubs // Mountaineering, climbing, trailrunning, triathlon etc. clubs
* The club is a registered organisation containing at least 4 athletes and present on social media
  • Training equipment
  • Equipment for creating training places
  • Branded equipment
  • Financial support for buying training gear
  • Financial support for buying equipment
  • Financial support to cover abroad training trips
Training spaces (climbing halls and other places for sport activities) // Support to climbing halls, water equipment rental stations, athletics halls, cross-country ski schools, etc. whose facilities or material resources were damaged as a result of military operations or completely lost due to the occupation
  • Equipment for creating training places
  • Financial support for rebuild and renovation
  • Financial support for activities held by the training space
  • Financial support for employees of the training space

National teams // Support for National teams of all types (climbing, running, trailrunning etc.)
* The team must have official status and be registered with its federation
  • Equipment and gear for training and competing
  • Financial support for abroad training trips
  • Financial support for competing

Athlete's families // Support to relatieves of athletes (par.1) who serve in Ukrainian army and/or were wounded at the battlefield or because of the war actions or died
  • Casual gear
  • Financial support for travelling, health issues or rehabilitation for children
  • Financial support for all other requests
Shops // Support to dealers who lost their shops or part of equipment due to war
  • Branded equipment
  • Financial support for rebuild and renovation
  • Casual gear
Competitions // Climbing, running, trail running, Nordic walking, cross-country skiing, alpine skiing or snowboarding, cycling competitions and cycling marathons.
* Competitions are defined as amateur competitions open to all or professional competitions included in the calendar of the relevant federation
  • Products of the corresponding brand for the prize fund
  • Financial assistance for the competition
  • Financing of the prize fund
  • Charitable contribution to the collection held by the event organizers
