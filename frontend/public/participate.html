Dear partners!
We are appealing to you to join our charity fund aimed at supporting and helping people and organizations involved in outdoor and cycling who have suffered and/or lost their property, the ability to do business/engage in their projects after the invasion of the Russian Federation.
Who does our foundation help? Affected athletes and their families, sports clubs and organizations, gyms, national teams, children's sports sections, competition organizers and businesses in the outdoor and cycling category to recover and develop after the devastating events and consequences caused by the attack of the Russian Federation. This resource will simplify the process of collecting requests from people/organizations in need of assistance. Requests will appear after our preliminary verification.
What types of assistance are in demand? material assistance in the form of products request for fundraising for the purchase of necessary equipment / supplies restoration of damaged property organization of trips to competitions/training for athletes from official federations abroad, etc. The full list of possible requests can be found here: Whom we Help.
How does it work? When you see a request that concerns you, please notify us by email, and we will help you organize the delivery/transfer of material assistance through our warehouse, or transfer financial assistance through the official bank accounts of our foundation with a subsequent report on the needs for which your assistance was directed. If you would like to offer your specific assistance in financial/material form or in any other way, please let us know and we will help you to select or find a request that will meet these conditions. For more information about our foundation and to discuss opportunities for cooperation, please contact us at fund@vysota.com.ua
Thank you in advance for your attention and support.
Glory to Ukraine!
Together to victory!
