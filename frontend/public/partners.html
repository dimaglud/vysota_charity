Many of you have expressed your unequivocal support for Ukraine and have already contributed to various types of financial and material assistance for Ukrainians, and we are very grateful for this help and support!
The main partner of the Foundation is the public fitness and sports organization "Federation of Mountaineering and Climbing of Ukraine", which is engaged in the development of mountaineering, rock climbing and other types of mountaineering. This partnership is an invaluable resource for restoring sports infrastructure and supporting those affected by the Russian Federation's attack.
