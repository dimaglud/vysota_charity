ABOUT FUND
Since 1998, we, the Vysota company, have been the official importer of many global brands, both outdoor and cycling (sportswear, outdoor equipment, bicycles and skiing). Since the beginning of Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, we have been actively helping both volunteers and the Armed Forces of Ukraine, responding to requests and shipping the necessary equipment.
Since we have been working in this field for a long time, a year and a half after the full-scale invasion, we set ourselves an additional goal - to establish a special charitable foundation to help people/organizations involved in outdoor and cycling.
The main goal of our foundation is to attract both financial and material assistance from foreign companies with which Vysota cooperates and redirect it to help to injured athletes and their families sports clubs and organizations, gyms, national teams, children's sports sections competition organizers outdoor and cycling businesses for recovery and development after the devastating events and consequences caused by the attack of the Russian Federation.
Most of our foreign partners have expressed their unconditional support for Ukraine and contributed to the best of their ability: they donated humanitarian aid in the form of their products, helped with fundraising and further procurement of necessary equipment. But they do not plan to stop there and are ready to continue their support. So with the help of our foundation, we plan to optimize and simplify the process of organizing and transferring the necessary assistance from well-known global brands directly to those in need.
